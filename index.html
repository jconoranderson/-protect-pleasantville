The Reality

The True Cost of Lennar's Project

The 900-Trip Reality

Lennar uses a "Phantom Office" baseline to claim a traffic decrease. In reality, 102 residential units plus gig-economy deliveries equals roughly 900 new daily trips funneling forcefully onto Bedford Road, mathematically eliminating safe turning gaps.

Hidden Taxpayer Burdens

The project dumps a 234% increase in wastewater (33,660 GPD) into our system with no peak-hour flow study. Furthermore, it adds 41-52 students, creating a sudden $1.13M+ annual instructional burden on the Pleasantville School District.

Severe Environmental Risks

Squeezing 102 units onto 15-35% steep, rocky slopes carries a "SEVERE" erosion hazard. Worse, the site plan subsidizes its drainage by dumping a 31,000-cubic-foot detention basin directly into neighboring infrastructure.
